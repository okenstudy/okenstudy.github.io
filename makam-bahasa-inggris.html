In The Cafetaria

Last week, Ulrich and Daniel are going to the cinema to watch a new movie. After that, they go home at 3.00 p.m. On the way home, they stopped at the cafetaria on Merpati street. Then, they entered the cafetaria. They sit on the corner there. "Hello", greet the waiter. " Hello.... I'd like two cup of Cappuccino please" said Ulrich. "Sure". The barista start to prepared the order.

While the coffee's prepared, Ulrich and Daniel are talking about the movie. After that, the waiter comes with two cup of coffe. " This is your", said the waiter. " Wait, i think you give us wrong kind of coffee. Is it cappuccino?" The waiter felt so guilty. " I'm sorry for my fault, i'll make the new one". The waiter bring the coffees back.

After few minutes later, the waiter back with new coffees. " Here you are, two cup of cappuccino". He served the coffees on the table. " Sorry, are you sure it is cappuccino?" ask Daniel. " I think you served the wrong coffees again. Capuccino doesn't like this." The waiter bring the coffees back again.

That's a long time, since the waiter go. Ulrich and Daniel are so bored. Then, Ulrich call the same waiter. " Sorry, we are waiting for the coffees too long. Can you serve it now?". " What coffees do you want? "ask the waiter. "Hey, i think we told you what coffee's that we want. Cappuccino please, " said Daniel. "Why don't you tell me early? There's no stock of Capuccino. I'm so sorry", said the waiter.

Ulrich and Daniel go out from the cafetaria soon. They annoyed with the bad service in the cafetaria. They promise that they won't to go there ever after.

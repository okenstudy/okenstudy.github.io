contoh: Magister Manajemen: M.M.

Penyelesaian:

[tex]\left|\begin{array}{cc}3x-1 & 3 \\ x+1 & x+2 \end{array}\right| = 0 \\ (3x-1)(x+2)-3(x+1) = 0 \\ 3x^2+5x-2-3x-3 = 0\\3x^2+2x-5 =0\\[/tex]

a = 3

b = 2

c = -5

[tex]\begin{aligned} x_1\times x_2 &= \frac{c}{a} \\ &= \frac{-5}{3} \\ &= -\frac{5}{3}\end{aligned}[/tex]

21. Sebutkan minimal 10 Distrik di Osaka!


Jawaban:

Hanya ada 5 distrik di Osaka dengan total 11 kota yang ada diantaranya, yaitu:

Kota Chihayaakasaka, Kanan, Mihara, dan Taishi di distrik Minamikawachi, Osaka.

Kota Shimamoto di distrik Mishima,Osaka.

Kota Tadaoka di distrik Senboku, Osaka.

Kota Kumatori, Misaki, dan Tajiri di distrik Sennan, Osaka.

Kota Nose dan Toyono di distrik Toyono, Osaka.

Penjelasan:

Distrik (郡 gun) saat ini merupakan unit geografis dan statistik yang terdiri dari satu atau beberapa desa di Jepang. Distrik berfungsi sebagai unit administrasi di Jepang pada tahun 1878 hingga 1921. Tidak ada satuan administratif di Indonesia yang sepadan dengan distrik di Jepang, tetapi secara umum hampir sama dengan county di Amerika Serikat.

Osaka is a charming, relaxed city best known for its food, fun and nightlife with some history and culture peeking through. Going to Osaka only takes a short shinkansen ride from Tokyo. Most of the people who live or were born in Osaka also use Kansai dialect/accent

Osaka

Osaka is a city in Japan located on the main island of Honshu. It is Japan's third largest city and capital of the western Kansai region. Osaka is well-known for its famous Castle or "Osaka-Jo" in Japanese language. It is a five-storey high castle and it is one of the most visually spectacular creations in the country because the castle is more than 450 years old.

There are thousands of ancient shrines, temples and castles, each with an interesting story to tell. Every year, travelers from within Japan and abroad make pilgrimages to many of these cultural landmarks to either pay respects, learn about Japanese history, or merely appreciate the stunningly intricate architecture for which Japan is globally renowned.

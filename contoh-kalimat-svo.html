4. we should give the children present, termasuk rumus apa? apakah menggunakan rumus SV, SVO, SVOO, SVOC, SVA, SVOA!​


Struktur kalimat "We should give the children presents" adalah:

Subject + Modal Verb + Verb 1 + Indirect Object + Direct Object.

[tex]\\[/tex]

PEMBAHASAN

Daripada menggunakan singkatan-singkatan seperti SPO, SVO, SPOK, dan sebagainya yang dapat membingungkan, lebih baik kamu memahami terlebih dahulu apa struktur dasar sebuah kalimat lengkap.

Kalimat lengkap adalah kalimat yang terdiri atas setidaknya subjek dan predikat. Subjek adalah orang, benda, makhluk hidup dsb. yang dibicarakan sementara predikat adalah apa deskripsi mengenai subjek.

Misalnya, dalam kalimat "Nama saya Edwin," subjeknya, yaitu hal yang dibicarakan, adalah "nama saya" dan predikatnya,  yaitu deskripsi tentang subjeknya, adalah "Edwin".

Dalam bahasa Inggris, predikat harus mengandung kata kerja. Sebagai contoh, dalam kalimat "My name is Edwin," predikatnya adalah is Edwin dengan is sebagai kata kerjanya.

Untuk contoh kalimat yang ditanyakan, strukturnya adalah: Subject + (Predicate + Object). Subjeknya adalah we, predikatnya adalah give the children presents, dan objeknya adalah the children sebagai objek tak langsung (indirect object) serta presents sebagai objek langsung (direct object).

Subject, Verb, Object, and Complement

Subject merupakan pelaku yang biasanya berupa kata benda yaitu, nama orang, nama tempat, nama benda, maupun kata gantinya.

Contoh:

Hany plays dolls.They ate pizza.My mother is cooking in the kitchen.

Verb merupakan kata kerja yang menyatakan tindakan atau keadaan.

Contoh:

I listen to the music.They play football.I'm a doctor.She is beautiful.

Object merupakan kata benda yang biasanya menerima tindakan. Biasanya mengikuti kata kerja di depannya.

Contoh:

I like banana.She opens the window.He gets a reward.

Complement merupakan kata keterangan yang menerangkan sebuah kata benda. Keterangan tersebut dapat berupa kata sifat, kata yang menjelaskan subject, tempat, waktu, cara, dan lain-lain.

Contoh:

I am tired.We will be eating at nine o'clock.She is a good teacher.

She folded clothes and put them neatly away.

Subject: sheVerb: folded & put awayObject: clothes & them

Jawaban:

Subject: she

Verb: folded, put

Object: clothes

Penjelasan:

"She" is the subject (S)

"Folded" and "Put" is the verb (V)

"Clothes" is the object (O)

I will explain about this things and give you examples.

"She folded clothes and put them neatly away"

• Subject is a person or thing that is being discussed, described, or dealt with.

In this sentence, "she" is the subject because it is the main topic of the sentence that is described. Other examples for subject is you, they, we, he, she, it.

• Verb is a word used to describe an action, state, or occurrence, and forming the main part of the predicate of a sentence. In this sentence, there are two verbs namely "folded" and "put". So it means that the subject (she) does 2 things. The first thing is folded. Folded means that she tidy up the clothes. And the 2nd thing is put. It's mean that she put the clothes to other place. Neatly isn't a verb. It's an adjective. Other examples of verb is eat, drink, sleep, study, walk, run.

• Object is a material thing that can be seen and touched. In this sentence, "clothes" is the object because it can be touched. Other examples of object is book, pen, pants, skirt, apple.

I will give you an examples.

• I eat an apple.

In this sentence, I is the subject, eat is the verb, and apple is the object.

• I learned about atoms yesterday.

In this sentence, I is the subject, learned is the verb, and atoms is the object.

• They went to Paris 2 years ago.

In this sentence, they is the subject, went is the verb, and Paris is the object.

11. 1. Buatlah/tulislah teks prosedur dalam Bahasa Inggris cara menanam bunga ( anggrek atau mawaratau bunga yang lain)​


Penjelasan:

Contoh teks prosedur bahasa inggris kali ini membahas tentang bagaimana menanam bunga adenium mulai dari benih.

Goal

This procedure text will explain step by step an easy way to grow adenium flower from seed.

Basically, the nature of adenium is in a desert area which is dry and hot. That is why this plant is also called desert rose. But in fact, we can grow it anywhere as long as this flower gets enough sunlight.

For the additional information, this plant does not like water too much, so you don’t need to give it water every day. Like any other desert plants, adenium can live without water for around two weeks in a pot.

Okay, to do this, you can prepare all the materials and follow the instruction bellow.

MaterialsAdenium fresh seedWarm waterWaterSunlightHeat 25-35 degrees CelsiusGood draining potting mixSeed tray

Steps

To Grow the Seeds

1. Soak the seeds in warm water for around 1-2 hours.

2. Fill the seed tray with draining potting mix.

3. Place each seed carefully at the tray. One layer for one seed.

4. Cover the seed with draining potting mix. Make sure that the seed is barely covered.

5. Water the seed using spray after you cover it with draining potting mix. Don’t give too much water.

6. Keep it at the place with good light source.

7. For the next treatment, spray your seed with water only when the draining potting mix is almost dry. It will be once a week or more. Remember to not too much giving water to the seed.

8. Your seed will sprout for 7-10 days. Each seed perhaps grows differently.

9. Give the same treatment until the seedlings have more than at least 8 leaves.

10. Move it to the larger pot. Do the same treatment. Normally, your adenium plants will start to have flower when they are 10 months or more.

Programming the Adenium Plant

Adenium will be beautiful plant if you have concept to program its growing.

Because it is different from any other plants, adenium plant will stuck and looks pity if you just give it water, fertilizer, and change the pot.

You need more action like pruning the leaves, cut the branches or even cut the root. Here some information you should know;

1. If you want to have adenium with big root or trunk, do pruning that is cutting the leaves frequently after the seedling at least has 3 months age. Do it every two months.

2. If you want your adenium has a lot of branches, just cut it. Every branch that you cut will has at least two new branches. The more your adenium has branches, the more flowers you will see when it starts blooming.

13. deskripsi bunga anggrek dalam bahasa inggris


The Orchidaceae are a diverse and widespread family of flowering plants, with blooms that are often colourful and often fragrant, commonly known as the orchid family.

Along with the Asteraceae, they are one of the two largest families of flowering plants. The Orchidaceae have about 28,000 currently accepted species, distributed in about 763 genera. The determination of which family is larger is still under debate, because verified data on the members of such enormous families are continually in flux. Regardless, the number of orchid species nearly equals the number of bony fishes and is more than twice the number of bird species, and about four times the number of mammal species. The family also encompasses about 6–11% of all seed plants. The largest genera are Bulbophyllum (2,000 species), Epidendrum (1,500 species), Dendrobium (1,400 species) and Pleurothallis (1,000 species).

The family also includes Vanilla (the genus of the vanilla plant), Orchis (type genus), and many commonly cultivated plants such as Phalaenopsis and Cattleya. Moreover, since the introduction of tropical species into cultivation in the 19th century, horticulturists have produced more than 100,000 hybrids and cultivars.

25. karakteristik tanaman anggrek dalam bahasa inggris dan artinya


Bahasa Inggris

Characteristics of Orchid Flowers

1. Root Section

The root of the orchid plant is a unique root because it sticks to the stems of other plants and usually follows the stem surface which is attached to the roots of the orchid flower. At the root there is a velamen tissue which functions to facilitate the roots to absorb water and do breathing, which is why no small root fibers are needed. Mushrooms are also useful for converting organic matter into topsoil and making food for the orchid.

2. Stem section

The stem of an orchid plant can grow tall vertically. Moon orchid flowers will appear on the armpits of the leaves. This orchid stem is very short and not like other orchids. At one point of growth it will consist of only one main stem that grows upwards.

3. Leaf Section

The leaves of the moon orchid flower usually grow around 5-10 cm. The leaves of this plant are usually mounted and lined up in two rows that are tight and face to face. This green leaf extends towards the tip of the leaf and its base squeezing the stem. The texture of this leaf is thick flesh because it contains chlorophyll, food cages and also water deposits.

4. Interest Section

The flower section consists of three flower petals and also three flower crowns. One fruit petal located on the back is called the back petal, while the other one is located on the side so that it is called the side leaf petals. The flower head itself consists of three fruits which are alternating between flower petals. At the center of the flower there are male and female genitals that are one unit. The moon orchid has three covers which include a cover parallel to the flower pole, side cover, and also the middle cover which has fine hairs. The moon orchid flower is very beautiful because it has a variety of color choices.

Artinya (Bahasa Indonesia)

Karakteristik Bunga Anggrek

1. Bagian Root

Akar tanaman anggrek adalah akar yang unik karena menempel pada batang tanaman lain dan biasanya mengikuti permukaan batang yang melekat pada akar bunga anggrek. Pada akar ada jaringan velamen yang berfungsi untuk memfasilitasi akar menyerap air dan bernafas, itulah sebabnya tidak diperlukan serat akar kecil. Jamur juga berguna untuk mengubah bahan organik menjadi humus dan membuat makanan untuk anggrek.

2. Bagian batang

Batang tanaman anggrek dapat tumbuh tinggi secara vertikal. Bunga anggrek bulan akan muncul di ketiak daun. Batang anggrek ini sangat pendek dan tidak seperti anggrek lainnya. Pada satu titik pertumbuhan hanya terdiri dari satu batang utama yang tumbuh ke atas.

3. Bagian Daun

Daun bunga anggrek bulan biasanya tumbuh sekitar 5-10 cm. Daun tanaman ini biasanya dipasang dan berbaris dalam dua baris yang rapat dan bertatap muka. Daun hijau ini memanjang ke arah ujung daun dan alasnya meremas batang. Tekstur daun ini adalah daging yang tebal karena mengandung klorofil, keramba makanan dan juga endapan air.

4. Bagian Bunga

Bagian bunga terdiri dari tiga kelopak bunga dan juga tiga mahkota bunga. Satu buah kelopak yang terletak di bagian belakang disebut kelopak belakang, sedangkan yang lainnya terletak di samping sehingga disebut kelopak daun samping. Kepala bunga itu sendiri terdiri dari tiga buah yang bergantian di antara kelopak bunga. Di tengah bunga ada alat kelamin laki-laki dan perempuan yang merupakan satu unit. Anggrek bulan memiliki tiga penutup yang meliputi penutup yang sejajar dengan tiang bunga, penutup samping, dan juga penutup tengah yang memiliki rambut halus. Bunga anggrek bulan sangat indah karena memiliki beragam pilihan warna.

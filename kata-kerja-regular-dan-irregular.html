Regular dan irregular verb adalah kata kerja dalam bahasa Inggris. Kalian dapat melihat perbedaannya saat kata tersebut berubah ke dalam bentuk past tense.


Regular verb adalah kata kerja reguler (biasa). Bentuk past tense dari kata kerja reguler cukup ditambahkan akhiran -ed.


Irregular verb adalah kebalikan dari regular verb, yaitu kata kerja yang tidak beraturan. Kata ini tidak mudah diprediksi dan tidak berakhiran -ed seperti regular verbs. Untuk mengetahui bentuk past tensenya, kalian harus menghapalkan satu persatu.


Pembahasan

Berikut adalah contoh regular dan irregular verb:


Regular verbs

Hug => Hugged - She hugged him, laying her head on his shoulder.Toss => Tossed - I just toss my dirty socks onto the floor.Dry => Dried - My skin is getting dry during the winter.Cry => Cried - I hate when my brother cry.Call => Called - My father called us yesterday from the Atlantic sea.Dance => Danced - And we danced whole night in the prom.Watch => Watched - I love to watch Netflix.Close => Closed - Could you please close the door?Believe => Believed - I believe you can do it!Hope => Hoped - We hope your meeting goes well.

Irregular verbs

Take => Took => Taken - Take it out, I don’t need it.Give => Gave => Given - A generous man gave his money to the beggar.Know => Knew => Known - I have known him whole my life.Say => Said => Said - My mother said don’t come home late.Sit => Sat => Sat - I sit in the front row of the class.Drink => Drunk => Drank - He was too drunk to realize.Run => Ran => Run - I want to run away from the house.Speak => Spoke => Spoken - My school principal spoke to me yesterday.Stand => Stood => Stood - I can’t stand lazybones.Teach => Taught = Taught - Amanda taught me how to speak english.

Rindy = 4/(Jumlah Perbandingan) × Jumlah Uang Mereka

Rindy = 4/(3+4) × 63.000

Rindy = 4/7 × 63.000

Rindy = 4 × 9.000

Rindy = 36.000

Jadi Banyak Uang Rindy adalah 36.000

S + to be + the + adj. + -est + C/O

S + to be + the + most + adj. + C/O (adjective terdiri dari dua suku kata atau lebih)

Diketahui =

Ibu rindy membeli 2 ekor ayam.

Berat kedua ekor ayam adalah... kg

[tex] = \frac{5}{4} kg[/tex]

Jadi, berat kedua ekor ayam adalah B. 5/4 kg

27. change these sentences below from direct into indirect speech1. they said we arrived before lunch 2. thomas said i was tired 3. the teacher said heeft class early 4. the man said to me I have harvested the mangoe5. karien said to me they will do their homework 6. rindy said I can do that 7. the guest said to me do you speak English 8. my teacher said to her is it yours ​


DIRECT-INDIRECT SPEECH

1) They said (that) they had arrived before lunch

2) Thomas said (that) he had been tired

3) The teacher said (that) he had left class early

4) The man said to me (that) he had harvested the mangoe

5) Karien said to me (that) they would do their homework

6) Rindy said (that) she could do that

7) The guest said to me if I spoke English

8) My teacher said to her if it was hers

EXPLANATION

Direct speech merupakan pernyataan yang disampaikan langsung oleh narasumber. Direct speech terpapar dalam bentuk percakapan dengan disertai tanda petik ganda ("). Sedangkan, Reported speech (indirect speech), merupakan pemberitaan ulang pernyataan yg disampaikan oleh narasumber kepada orang lain di kurun waktu berbeda dengan menggunakan bahasa sendiri, tanpa mengubah informasi yg tersirat.

Perubahan bentuk noun clause verb pada reported speech bergantung pada bentuk reporting verb dan jenis informasi yg disampaikan pada direct speech, apakah berupa informasi faktual, imperative, interrogative dan prohibition.

Jika reporting verb berbentuk lampau { said, told, asked }, noun clause verb mengalami backshift berikut ini :

➻Simple present ➛Simple past

➻Simple past ➛Past perfect

➻Present perfect ➛Past perfect

➻Simple future ➛ Present conditional

➻Present continuous ➛Past continuous

➻Past continuous ➛Past perfect continuous

Perubahan ini tidak berlaku, jika yg dibicarakan adalah kebenaran umum (general truth). Meskipun, reporting verb-nya bentuk lampau, noun clause verb-nya akan tetap sama berbentuk simple present karena yg diulas adalah kebenaran/fenomena.

Yang perlu diperhatikan, dalam merubah direct speech ke indirect speech, yaitu :

1. Jenis reporting verb yg digunakan

2. Jenis informasi yg disampaikan, apakah beruba imperative, interrogative, exclamation.

3. Pronoun (kata ganti orang), posisi orang yang terlibat.

4. Verb tense

5. Adverb of time (keterangan waktu)

6. Adverb of place (keterangan tempat/petunjuk)

UNCLE TED’S ADVENTURE

My uncle Ted (1) was a great movie star. He once (2) told me about his artistic career. It (3) began some twenty years ago when he (4) went to Hollywood. They (5) chose my uncle to play in a new Tarzan movie. They (6) spent a lot of money on that movie. He (7) fought against some natives. He (8) rode an angry elephant. He (9) fell from a tall tree into a dangerous river. He (10) swam very fast across it because of the crocodiles. Once a lion almost (11) caught him. Luckily someone (12) killed the animal just in time. On another occasion, he (13) felt something moving behind him. He (14) thought it was a snake. He (15) ran but (16) did not see a big hole in front of him. He (17) broke his leg. They (18) drove him to a hospital nearby. Uncle Ted (19) left the hospital a month later. He (20) became famous after the movie. After that, he (21) made more than twenty adventure movies. Last year he (22) flew to Rio on his vacation. He (23) took a taxi at the airport. He (24) met a beautiful carioca . We never (25) saw uncle Ted again. ​

8. Perbandingan usia Anggun dan Beti sekarang 3:2. Empat tahun yang lalu usia beti8 tahun. Dua tahun yang akan datang selisih umur Anggun dan betiA. 6C. 18B 12D. 20harap jawab ya ​


Jawaban:

Anggun : Beti = 3 : 2

Empat tahun lalu umur beti = 8 tahun, maka umur beti sekarang adalah = 8 + 4 = 12 tahun.

sehingga umur anggun sekarang adalah:

[tex]umur \: anggun = \frac{3}{2} \times 12 \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: = 3 \times 6 \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: = 18 \: tahun[/tex]

maka selisih umur anggun dan beti dua tahun yang akan datang:

= umur anggun - umur betu

= (18 + 2) - (12 + 2)

= 20 - 14

= 6 tahun

jadi selisih umur mereka 2 tahun yang akan datang adalah 6 tahun.

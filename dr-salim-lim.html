10. bantuin dong A) Lim x->3 dr X³-27/x²-9 B) lim x->8 dr x-8/³√x -2


a. Lim x->3  (X³-27/x²-9)
X³ - 27 = (x-3)(x² + 3x + 9)
x² - 9 = (x-3)(x+3)
jadi Lim x->3  (X³-27/x²-9) = Lim x->3  ((x-3)(x² + 3x + 9)/(x-3)(x+3))
Lim x->3 (x² + 3x + 9)/(x+3) = (3² + 3(3) + 9)/(3+3) = 27/6 = 9/2
b. lim x->8 (x-8/³√x -2)
penyebut dan pembilang diturunkan 
turunan x-8 = 1
turunan (x)^1/3 - 2 = 1/(3(x)^2/3)
jadi hasilnya = 1/(1/(3(8)^2/3) = 1/ (1/12) = 12

29. minta tlong d bantu cri hasil dr lim (1+cos 2x) dan lim 3x tan 2x / 1- cos 4x


Pertama:
[tex]\displaystyle \lim_{x\to0}1+\cos 2x=1+\cos2(0) \\ \lim_{x\to0}1+\cos 2x=1+\cos 0 \\ \lim_{x\to0}1+\cos 2x=1+1 \\ \lim_{x\to0}1+\cos 2x=2[/tex]
Kedua,
[tex]$\begin{align}\lim_{x\to0}\frac{3x\tan 2x}{1-\cos 4x}&=\lim_{x\to0}\frac{3x\tan2x}{1-(1-2\sin^22x)} \\ &=\lim_{x\to0}\frac{3x\tan2x}{2\sin^22x} \\ &=\frac{3}{2}\lim_{x\to0}\frac{x\tan 2x}{\sin^22x} \\ &=\frac{3}{2}\lim_{x\to0}\frac{x}{\sin2x}\times\lim_{x\to0}\frac{\tan 2x}{\sin2x} \\ &=\frac{3}{2}\times\frac{1}{2}\times 1 \\ \lim_{x\to0}\frac{3x\tan 2x}{1-\cos 4x}&=\frac{3}{4}\end{align}[/tex]

4. If knew his number, i ( call ) him


║CONDITIONAL SENTENCE║

Answer

If I knew his number, I would call him

Discussion

Bentuk If-conditional di atas menggunakan tipe ke dua, bisa dicermati pada bentuk past pada kata kerja di IF-Clause nya "knew", maka, untuk main clause -nya menggunakan rumus seperti berikut :

If + S + Verb 2 + O/C , Subject + would + verb 1 + O/C

Pernyataan conditional di atas merefleksikan bahwa terdapat kontras dengan kondisi kenyataannya. Si pembicara memiliki niatan tetapi kondisi/persyaratannya tidak terpenuhi, sehingga terjadinya kontras dengan faktanya atau kita sebut sebagai andai-andai.

Explanation

CONDITIONAL SENTENCE/ ANDAI-ANDAI

Conditional sentence tells about the condition that will be actualized ( possible condition ) or only dreaming ( intention ) because of the particular condition.

Conditional sentence structure consists -If clause and result clause ( main clause ).

Use Comma when the result clause (main clause ) is started at the beginning sentence. Otherwise, there is no comma when -IF clause is put after the result clause.

There are 4 types of Conditional Sentence

1. CONDITIONAL ZERO

Conditional zero is used to describe the PHENOMENON,  and GENERAL TRUTH ( THE FACT/REALITY  )Conditional zero uses the simple present tense form to reflect the truth.

Pattern :

IF+S+base verb (s/es ) + O/C , S+base verb (s/es ) + O/C

Example :

If it rains , the roads get wet

Analysis

It tells about the general truth or phenomenon.

It uses the simple present tense form to describe the truth. We can't use simple future in its main clause (result clause ), because it is more than just a prediction. It is proven that the roads always get wet after raining.

2. CONDITIONAL TYPE 1

Conditional type 1 is used to describe the truth  in the present/future ( The planning (prediction)  will be probably happened )In fact , the speaker will actualize his/her dream now or soon .

Pattern :

If +S+ verb (s/es) + O, S+will+verb1+O/C

Example :

If the students study hard, they will pass the exam easily.

Analysis

Fact : it's possible that they will be successful for the exam later because they study hard.

It uses simple future form in its main clause / result clause to describe the possible condition and state the prediction will be true.

3. CONDITIONAL TYPE 2

Conditional type 2 is used to describe  the untruth in the present / futureIt is Contrary to the fact in the present or it could be in the futureThe speaker has an intention ( dream ) but he/she can't afford his/her dream comes true in the present/future

"Keep in mind, if we use the first , second person and third person singular  ( I, she, he, it , ) , it must be followed by the PAST linking verb "were."" It is the same way to the plural form as the formal writing." If we use the PAST linking verb "was " , it should be used in the colloquial as the informal conversation.

Pattern:

If+S+verb2+O/C , S+Would/Could+verb1+O/C

Example :

If I were a bird, I would fly around the world.

Analysis

Fact in the present/future : I can't fly around the world because I'm not a bird now.

It uses the simple past form to reflect the untruth condition in the present and the speaker's intention will not come true because it is contrary to the real condition for now or future.

4. CONDITIONAL TYPE 3

Conditional type 3 Is used to describe  the untruth in the pastContrary to the fact in the past

Pattern :

If+S+Had+Verb 3+O/C  , S+Would+have+Verb 3+O/C

Example :

If I had found my car key, I would have fetched my grandmother at the airport.

Analysis :

Fact in the past  : I didn't find my car key, so I didn't fetch my grandmother at the airport

It reflects a regret for something that we couldn't afford to do in the past.

8. apa bedanya know, knew, known?


Perbedaan dari know, knew, dan known adalah know merupakan bentuk verb 1, knew merupakan bentuk verb 2 dan known merupakan bentuk verb 3. Know, knew, dan known merupakan kata kerja tidak beraturan sehingga memiliki bentuk yang berbeda. Sementara dari segi maknanya sama, yaitu mengetahui.

Pembahasan

Verb merupakan bentuk kata kerja. Dalam English verbs mempunyai empat bentuk yaitu :

Simple form [verb 1].Simple past [verb 2].Past participle [verb 3].Present participle [verb.ing].

Untuk mengubah bentuk dari verb 1 ke verb 2 atau verb 3, harus diketahui terlebih dahulu apakah termasuk kata kerja beraturan [regular verbs] atau kata kerja tidak beratuan [irregular verbs]. Untuk mengetahui perbedaanya simak penjelasan berikut :

Regular verbs merupakan kata kerja beraturan. Untuk mengubah bentuk dari verb 1 ke verb 2 atau verb 3 hanya perlu ditambahi akhiran –ed. Contoh :

Verb 1 >> Verb 2 >> Verb 3

Clean >> Cleaned >> Cleaned

Rob >> Robbed >> Robbed

Lie >> Lied >> Lied

Irregular verbs merupakan kata kerja tidak beraturan. Biasanya tidak diakhiri dengan akhiran –ed. Untuk mengubah bentuk dari verb 1 ke verb 2 atau verb 3 harus melihat kamus terlebih dahulu. Untuk mempelajarinya biasanya dengan sistem menghafal. Contoh :

Verb 1 >> Verb 2 >> Verb 3

Eat >> Ate >> Eaten

Buy >> Bought >> Bought

Write >> Wrote >> Written

Dari pembahahasan diatas maka diketahui bahwa know merupakan a Irregular verb. Maka perbedaan ketiganya adalah know merupakan bentuk verb 1, knew merupakan bentuk verb 2 dan known merupakan bentuk verb 3.

21. Hardin scott… tessa young since the first year of college. select one: a. has knew b. have knew c. have known d. has known e. knew


▪ Present Perfect

(d) has known

└▹ Hardin Scott has known Tessa Young since the first year of college.

PENDAHULUAN

Present perfect tense adalah jenis kalimat yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang bermula dari masa lalu namun masih berlanjut atau berhubungan sampai sekarang.

Present perfect tense juga berguna untuk menyebutkan tindakan atau peristiwa yang terjadi beberapa kali.

๑ rumus/pola ๑

-kalimat verbal-

(+) S + have/has + verb 3 + O/C(-) S + have/has + not + verb 3 + O/C(?) Have/has + s + verb 3 + O/C + ?

-kalimat nominal-

(+) S + have/has + been + O/C(-) S + have/has + not + been + O/C(?) Have/has + s + been + O/C + ?

๑ contoh penggunaan ๑

-kalimat verbal-

(+) Tessa has met him before. (-) Tessa hasn't met him before. (?) Has Tessa met him before?

-kalimat nominal-

(+) Tessa has been a student since she was young. (-) Tessa hasn't been a student since she was young. (?) Has Tessa been a student since she was young?

PEMBAHASAN

Pada soal, diminta untuk melengkapi kalimat dengan pilihan jawaban yang tepat.

Hardin Scott ___ Tessa Young since the first year of college.

select one:

a. has knew b. have knewc. have known d. has knowne. knew

Hardin Scott ___ Tessa Young since the first year of college.

Kalimat di atas termasuk pola kalimat present perfect, salah satunya karena terdapat adverb ‘since’ (sejak), yang berarti kejadian itu berawal dari masa lalu dan masih berhubungan hingga kini. Jadi, bagian kosongnya mesti dilengkapi dengan have/has + verb 3.Subjek ‘Hardin’ termasuk subjek he/she/it, maka yang digunakan adalah has. Verb 3 dari kata kerja know adalah known.

→ Hardin Scott has known Tessa Young since the first year of college.

Maka, opsi jawaban yang benar adalah:

d. has known

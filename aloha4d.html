= 12 × 12 × 12 - 6 × 6 + 10

= 144 × 12 - 36 + 10

= 1.728 - 36 + 10

= 1.692 + 10

= 1.702

8² × 2 - 17

= 8 × 8 × 2 - 17

= 64 × 2 - 17

= 128 - 17

= 111

Soal :

1. 12³ - 6² + 10 = ....?

2. 8² × 2 - 17 = ....?

Pembahasan

1. (12 × 12) - (6 × 6) + 10 =

= (144 - 36) + 10

= 108 + 10

= 118

2. (8 × 8) × 2 - 17 =

= (64 × 2) - 17

= 128 - 17

= 111

Persegi
K ABCD = 60 cm
s PQRS = s ABCD + 3 cm

a) K ABCD = 4s
60 = 4s
60/4 = s
15 cm = s
L ABCD = s^2
= 15^2
= 225 cm^2

b) s PQRS = s ABCD + 3 cm
= 15 + 3
= 18 cm
L PQRS = s^2
= 18^2
= 324 cm^2

c) L PQRS - L ABCD
= 324 - 225
= 99 cm^2

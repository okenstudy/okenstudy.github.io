24. A : what day is it today ? B : Today Is . . . . A : What date is it today ? B : Today is . . . 5 AGUSTUS 2010 - KAMIS


A : What day is today ?

B : Today is Thursday

A : What date is it today ?

B : Today is Thursday the 5th of August, 2010

Pembahasan

Date written in British English :

Day followed by the month, then followed by the year.

Examples:

5 August 5 August 20105th August 2010the 5th August 2010the 5th of August 2010 (more formal)the 5 of August, 2010 (more formal)

If you add "the" and "of", you must add both of them. It is incorrect to say only 5th of August or the the 5th August.

If you want to add the name of the day, it comes before the date, and be separated by a comma or joined by “the” and “of.”

Examples ;

Thursday, 5 August  2010Thursday the 5th of August, 2010

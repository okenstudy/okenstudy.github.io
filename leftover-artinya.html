10. 23. lixin has three ribbons of lengths 160 cm, 192 cm and 240 cm respectively. she wishes to cut all the ribbon into equal number of pieces without any leftover ribbons. find (i) the largest possible length of each piece of ribbons, (ii) the total number of pieces of ribbons that lixin has cut.


Jawaban:

(i) the largest possible length of each piece of ribbon.

a. ribbon with the length 160 cm = 10 cm

b. ribbon with the length 192 cm = 12 cm

c. ribbon with the length 240 cm = 15 cm

(ii) the total number of pieces of ribbons that Lixin has cut is 48 pieces.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

To find into how many pieces all ribbons can be cut, we use greatest common divisor.

Integer factorization of 160

160 = 2 x 2 x 2 x 2 x 2 x 5 = 2⁵ x 5

Integer factorization of 192

192 = 2 x 2 x 2 x 2 x 2 x 2 x 3 = 2⁶ x 3

Integer factorization of 240

240 = 2 x 2 x 2 x 2 x 3 x 5 = 2⁴ x 3 x 5

Greatest common divisor of 160, 192, and 240 is

2⁴ = 16

(i) the largest possible length of each piece of ribbon.

a. ribbon with the length 160 cm = 160 cm ÷ 16 = 10 cm

b. ribbon with the length 192 cm = 192 cm ÷ 16 = 12 cm

c. ribbon with the length 240 cm = 240 cm ÷ 16 = 15 cm

(ii) the total number of pieces of ribbons that Lixin has cut.

All three ribbons are cutting into 16 pieces. Then, total pieces of ribbon is

3 x 16 = 48 pieces.

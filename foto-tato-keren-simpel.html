23. Simpel past tense ubah kalimat yang ada di foto jadi positif,negatif dan pertanyaan


Question:

1. They ... (go) to the beach on weekend.

Answer:

(+) They went to the beach on weekend.

(-) They didn't/did not go to the beach on weekend.

(?) Did they go to the beach on weekend?

Question:

2. They ... (enjoy) the sunset.

Answer:

(+) They enjoyed the sunset.

(-) They didn't/did not enjoy  the sunset.

(?) Did they enjoy  the sunset?

Question:

3. We ...  (like) to make a big sand castle.

Answer:

(+) We liked to make a big sand castle.

(-) We didn't/did not like to make a big sand castle.

(?) Did they like to make a big sand castle?

Question:

4. I ... (read) one book every month.

Answer:

(+) I read one book every month.

(-) I didn't/did not read one book every month.

(?) Did you read one book every month?

Question:

5. You ... (tell) a fairy tale before sleeping.

Answer:

(+) You told a fairy tale before sleeping.

(-) You didn't/did not tell a fairy tale before sleeping.

(?) Did I tell a fairy tale before sleeping?

Question:

6. Anita ... (go) to Kusamba Market at 5 a.m.

Answer:

(+) She went to Kusamba Market at 5 a.m.

(-) She didn't/did not go to Kusamba Market at 5 a.m.

(?) Did she go to Kusamba Market at 5 a.m.?

Question:

7. She always ... (enjoy) her trip to that market.

Answer:

(+) She always enjoyed her trip to that market.

(-) She always didn't/did not enjoy  her trip to that market.

(?) Did she always enjoy her trip to that market?

Question:

8. Harry ...  (read) a newspaper before working.

Answer:

(+) He read a newspaper before working.

(-) He didn't/did not read a newspaper before working.

(?) Did he read a newspaper before working?

Question:

9. This magazine ... (tell) me how to be creative.

Answer:

(+) It told me how to be a creative.

(-) It didn't/did not tell me how to be a creative.

(?) Did it tell me how to be a creative?

Question:

10. My dog ... (like) to play after eating.

Answer:

(+) It liked to play after eating.

(-) It didn't/did not like to play after eating.

(?) Did it like to play after eating?

Formula:

(+) Subject + past tense.

(-) Subject + didn't/did not + infinitive.

(?) Did + subject + infinitive?

Note : Remeber this all formulas to solve the Past Tense problem.

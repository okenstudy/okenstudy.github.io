A. pukul 10.00

B. pukul 10.15

C. pukul 10.30

jarak tempuh nanda: 275 – x

kecepatan sinta: 45km/jam

kecepatan nanda: 65km/jam

waktu tempuh sinta: Ts

waktu tempuh nanda: Tn

Ts = Tn

x/45 = 275 – x / 65

65 x = 12.375 – 45 x

110 x = 12.375

x = 12.375 / 110

Ts = x / 45

= 12.375 / 110 ÷ 45

= 12.375 / 110 × 1/45 = 2,5 jam

waktu berpapasan = 08.30 + 2,5 jam = 11.00

[tex]A = \frac{P \: . \: L}{R} \\ [/tex]

[tex]P = \frac{R \: . \: A}{L} \\ [/tex]

[tex]L = \frac{R \: . \: A}{P} \\ [/tex]

R = Hambatan kawat (ohm)

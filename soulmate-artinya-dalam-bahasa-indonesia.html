14. Hadi "soulmate" putra menembakkan peluru plastik dengan kecepatan awal 120 m/s membentuk sudut elevasi 30° terhadap permukaan tanah. Jika g = 10 m/s2, peluru mencapai titik tertinggi setelah ….


diketahui : v₀ = 120 m/s
                 θ  = 30°
                 g  = 10 m/s²
ditanya : t = ....?
dijawab :

t = v₀ sin θ / g
  = 120 sin (30°) / 10
  = 12 x (1/2)
t = 6 s

jadi peluru mencapai titik tertinggi setelah 6 sekon

PEMBAHASAN

Change into passive.

=> Ubahlah ke dalam Passive.

Berikut Berdasarkan Rumus:

Past Tense => yesterday, this morning Active: S + V2 + OPassive: O + Be (was/were) + V3 + By S

1. My father repaired the car yesterday

➡️The car was repaired by my father yesterday

5. This morning my friends and I met miss.adis in the café.

➡️ This morning, Miss adis was met by my friends and me in the cafe

Catatan:No. 1 dan 5 menggunakan Past Tense karena ada ciri waktu lampau "yesterday" (kemarin) dan "this morning" (pagi ini/tadi pagi)Jadi, pola Passive adalah Be (was/were) + V3

Present Tense Active: S + V1 + OPassive: O + Be (is/am/are) + V3 + By S

2. The students play a football in the field with other team

➡️ A Football is played by the students in the field other team

3. I study english with miss.Olive about passive voice.

➡️ English is studied by me with Miss. Olive about passive Voice

4. The brother reads a magazine in the garden with her soulmate.

➡️ A magazine is read by the brother in the garden

Catatan: No. 2-3-4 tidak ada keterangan waktu spesifik maka menggunakan Tenses yg umum yaitu Present TenseJadi, pola Passive adalah Be (is/am/are) + V3

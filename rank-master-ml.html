Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Hasil akar-akar

= 1 . (-5/3)

= -5/3

Kode Kategorisasi : 11.2.5

Kelas 11

Pelajaran 2 - Matematika

Bab 5 - Matriks

[tex]\left|\begin{array}{cc}3x-1 & 3 \\ x+1 & x+2 \end{array}\right| = 0 \\ [/tex]

[tex]\left|\begin{array}{cc}3x-1 & 3 \\ x+1 & x+2 \end{array}\right| = 0 \\ (3x-1)(x+2)-3(x+1) = 0 \\ 3x^2+5x-2-3x-3 = 0\\3x^2+2x-5 =0\\[/tex]

a = 3

b = 2
